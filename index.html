Fettuccine Alfredo Carbonara Risotto Barilla

Fettuccine pasta is a long, strand-like pasta that is often served al dente with a creamy, dairy-based sauce.

Alfredo is a creamy dreamy pan sauce made from butter and Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese.

Carbonara is a rich sauce made from eggs and pecorino, coating spaghetti and salty bites of guanciale, finished with black pepper.

Risotto is an Italian rice dish cooked with broth until it reaches a creamy consistency.

Barilla is used by world leader in the markets of pasta and ready–to–use sauces in continental Europe, bakery products in Italy and crispbread in Scandinavia.
